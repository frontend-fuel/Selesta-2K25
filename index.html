Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate? +

All college students are eligible to participate. Teams must have 2-4 members, and all team members should be from the same institution. Participants should be currently enrolled students.

What should I bring? +

Please bring your laptop. You may also bring any specific hardware if your project requires it.

Are there any theme restrictions? +

Projects must align with one of our five tracks: AI Environmental Monitor, Smart Education Platform, AI Health Assistant, Smart Agriculture System, or Student Innovation Track. Your solution should be original and developed during the hackathon.

How will projects be judged? +

Projects will be evaluated based on innovation (30%), technical complexity (25%), practical impact (25%), and presentation quality (20%). Each team will have 5 minutes to present their solution to our panel of industry experts and academic judges.
